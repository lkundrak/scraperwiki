Community Manager

Planning applications, mobile phone bills, gas wholesale flows…

ScraperWiki is a funded startup in the Liverpool Knowledge Quarter, UK, liberating the world’s data.

We are looking for a community manager to help us to grow and develop our data scientist community. These are coders across the world who are getting, cleaning and analysing everything - from their own data to government data.

You will need to code and want to to encourage others to help us with our mission to liberate data.

You will be confident using social media, communicating through blogs and forums and you will also want to tell the world about the amazing work that our community is doing to help make people’s lives better.

You will manage, attend and speak at national and international events and work with other organisations in the big data, data science, open data and developer communities to ensure that we are working together as a bigger voice.

You will report to the CEO of ScraperWiki.

We would like you to be based at the ScraperWiki office in the Liverpool Knowledge Quarter. The role will involve travelling to events.

To apply - send the following:

  • A story of one time you’ve built a community
  • A link to a scraper (of any quality!) that you’ve made on ScraperWiki
  • A link to your résumé/CV
  • Any questions you have about the job
